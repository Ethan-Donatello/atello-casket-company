About Us

The Atello Casket Company is South Africa's trusted wholesale supplier of premium caskets, proudly based in Johannesburg and serving funeral homes nationwide.

With a focus on quality, dignity, and reliability, we specialize in providing elegantly crafted caskets that meet the highest standards of craftsmanship and presentation. Whether traditional or contemporary, our carefully curated range is designed to meet the diverse needs of families and funeral professionals alike.

Operating entirely remotely, we’ve streamlined the ordering and delivery process to ensure efficient, discreet, and dependable service — wherever you are in South Africa. This remote model allows us to keep overheads low and pass the value directly to our clients without compromising on the premium standards we’re known for.

At The Atello Casket Company, we understand that we’re not just supplying a product — we’re supporting a moment of meaning. That’s why our commitment to excellence, respect, and professional partnership is at the heart of everything we do.


Material And Skills


At Atello Casket Company, we keep enough raw materials on hand to cover 3 to 4 months of production.
This helps us respond quickly to changes in demand without delays.

Being based in Johannesburg gives us access to a strong pool of skilled workers.
Our team is also multi-skilled, which means we can easily shift staff around to keep production running smoothly.
This allows us to maintain high quality and deliver on time, every time.


Production And Storage


Our production line is designed for flexibility, making it easy to adjust for various casket sizes and finishes.

We also create custom caskets based on specific requests. When demand is high or deadlines are tight, we can quickly move to a 24-hour production schedule by operating in three shifts.
The layout of our multi-level factory also helps us store finished caskets securely and efficiently.
